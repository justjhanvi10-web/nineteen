The Nineteen Story

This Is Your
Becoming.

Nineteen was born from a simple, undeniable truth: the years spent becoming are just as sacred as the destination.

It is for the ones building businesses from bedrooms at midnight. Studying under lamplight while the world sleeps. Creating art the world hasn't seen yet. Chasing every opportunity with everything they have. It is for the ones who feel it — that quiet, burning belief that something great is ahead.

We made Nineteen for that feeling. For the mornings that feel like possibility. For the evenings that feel like freedom. For the in-between moments that make up a life worth living.

This is not clothing. This is armor for the ambitious. Ease for the relentless. Comfort for the ones who refuse to stop.

The name is a chapter. A state of being. That moment when potential stops being a promise and starts becoming a reality. Whether you are nineteen or thirty-nine — if you are still building, still dreaming, still becoming — this is for you.

"The world will remember the ones who refused to wait for permission."

The Collection Story

Why After Hours
Exists.

There is a version of luxury that demands performance. That asks you to look the part, play the role, and suppress the parts of yourself that are still figuring it out.

After Hours is not that.

After Hours was designed for the hours that belong to you — the ones between obligations, before the world wakes, after the calls end. The ones where you are allowed to simply be.

"Comfort can become confidence. Ease can become elegance. What you wear when no one is watching shapes who you become when everyone is."

Luxury today means ease. Simplicity. Intention. Fabric that moves with you, silhouettes that flatter without effort, details that whisper rather than shout.

After Hours was built not to distract you from your life — but to support it completely.

Every Detail Intentional

Designed, Not Assembled.

Each design choice in After Hours was made with one purpose: to create a garment that disappears into your life and elevates everything around it.

⌒
Off-Shoulder Neckline

A neckline that holds without holding back. Designed to sit with natural ease — effortlessly relaxed, quietly refined. The geometry of comfort made visible.

◁
Oversized Sleeves

Cut long and full to create that signature After Hours drape. Volume with intention. The kind of sleeve that makes every gesture feel considered.

✦
Embroidered 19 Logo

The 19 mark, placed with precision and executed with craft. Tonal embroidery that reads like a quiet confidence — a signature for those who know.

▽
Flared Fit

A silhouette that begins relaxed and opens with intention. The flare at the hem is architectural — drawing the eye downward, elongating naturally, adding movement without noise.

◈
Soft Premium Fabric

Heavyweight fleece that carries its weight beautifully — dense enough to drape, soft enough to forget you're wearing it. Fabric that improves with time, not despite it.

○
Elastic Waistband

Engineered to move with you, not against you. Sits without pinching. Adjusts without effort. Comfort that doesn't announce itself — it simply disappears so you can be present.
